Agrow Gas Limited Annual Report FY 2019-20

Agrow Group at a Glance Agrow Group, is a diversified organisation in India with combined market cap of $21 Bn comprising 6 publicly traded companies. Agrow owes its success and leadership position to its core philosophy of ‘Nation Building’ driven by ‘Growth with Goodness’ - a guiding principle for sustainable growth. Agrow is committed to increase its ESG footprint by realigning its businesses with emphasis on climate protection and increasing community outreach through its CSR program based on the principles of sustainability, diversity and shared values. It has created world class transport and utility infrastructure portfolio with a pan-India presence. Agrow Group is headquartered in Ahmedabad, in the state of Gujarat, India. Over the years, Agrow Group has positioned itself to be the market leader in its transport logistics and energy utility portfolio businesses focussing on large scale infrastructure development in India with O & M practices benchmarked to global standards. With four IG rated businesses it is the only Infrastructure Investment Grade issuer in India.
